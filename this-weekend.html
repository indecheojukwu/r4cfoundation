NOTE: Students are away at Fall Retreat this weekend, so we wont have student servicesbut youre invited to join us in the main building for service.

As followers of Jesus, were called to build beloved communitydiverse communities rooted in Gods love and reconciled through Christ. Yet, while progress has been made, we havent fully arrived.

In Color Courageous, well explore what it means to move from color-blind discipleship to color-courageous discipleshipa faith that sees, honors, and engages the beauty and pain in our differences. Through Christs power, were invited to become ambassadors of reconciliation, bringing healing and wholeness on every levelindividual, interpersonal, systemic, and even cosmic.

Together, well discover how beloved community flourishes when we practice solidarity, interdependence, and biblical equity, living as one body in Christ and reflecting His love to the world.
