5 Steps to Your First Visit

1. Pick a Service Time

  • Sunday at 9:30am
  • Sunday at 11:30am

2. Map it

The Vineyard is located in Tri-County off Kemper Rd. across from Target.
Address: 11340 Century Circle E., Cincinnati, OH 45246

Get Directions

3. Check In the Kids

If you arrive with kids, stop by the New Family Check In desk located in the Family Entrance Lobby. Our team will need a few minutes to register your child and set them up for a great kid's church experience, so plan to arrive about 10-15 minutes early.

New Family Registration

Kids Info

Students Info

4. Join the Service

Come on in! Each service lasts a little over one hour. We have a few brief announcements, a time of singing, prayer and a 30-minute message.

This Weekend

5. Visit The Welcome Center

After each service, our Welcome Center is available with volunteers and Pastors to answer all of your questions, help you find your way around, and make you feel at home. They also have a gift ready for you! Just say, "I'm new!"

FAQ

What do people wear?

Most people dress very casually, but the most important thing is that you feel comfortable...we're pretty sure what you wear doesn't matter to God, so it doesn't to us either!

Do you accommodate the handicapped?

Yes, there is seating for the handicapped located in the balcony and on the first floor. There's an elevator located in the Main Lobby, which you can take down to the first floor.

What do you have for kids and teens?

Infants - 5th Grade
Our Vineyard Kids ministry provides age-appropriate weekend experiences for infants through 5th grade. Vineyard Kids is fun and engaging with activities and basic Bible lessons your child will love. Meets Sundays at 9:30 & 11:30am in the Main Building.

6th-12th Graders
Vineyard Students helps teens grow in their relationship with God, build lifelong friendships with other teens, and learn from adult leaders who share a biblical perspective. Meets Sundays at 11:30am in the Student Union building.

More About Students

Where can I take communion or receive prayer?

Prayer is offered after every service. You can receive prayer from the prayer teams located by the stage or in the front of the balcony.

Communion elements are available throughout the auditorium at every service

What do you believe?

Vineyard Cincinnati is open to everyone -- no matter what your thoughts are about God or church.

Whether you’re new to church or been around your whole life, you're in good company with those of us who are exploring who God is -- or rediscovering what church can be.
