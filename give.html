Why give?

We believe that our time, energy, strengths, thoughts, relationships, work, and money are all gifts from God, and that we have been given the opportunity to manageor stewardthese resources.

Our hope is that the Vineyard is, or becomes, your spiritual home where you can grow in your walk with the Lord!

We encourage those just beginning this journey to simply pray about where to start, and then grow.

Text To Give

Simply text your amount to +254700000

You will need to complete a one-time setup screen on your phone. If you donate by text, you don't need to fill out any additional registration forms. Thank you!

Types of gifts you can make

  • one-time credit/debit card contributions
  • recurring credit/debit card
  • eCheck/EFT/ACH giving plans

Payment Via Paybill

To give your donation, simply use our Paybill through M-Pesa. Go to Lipa na M-Pesa, select Paybill, enter our Paybill Number, and use your name or Donation as the account number. Once you confirm the amount and send, your contribution goes directly to supporting our sports empowerment and church programs.
